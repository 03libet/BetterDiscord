    FAQ

    Answers to general questions people asked frequently.

    Can't Access Settings

    How to access Discords settings manually because of a theme/plugin problem.

    • Step 1: Do CTRL+, on your keyboard

    • Step 2: Disable/Remove the recently added Plugin/Theme/CSS

    • Step 3: Do CTRL+R on your keyboard

    Removed a theme but still shows up

    This is what we call a Ghost Theme it's a small bug in BetterDiscord.

    WARNING: This will erase all of your themes!

    • Step 1: Restart your Discord by doing CTRL+R just to start clean.

    • Step 2: Do CTRL+SHIFT+I on your keyboard

    • Step 3: Click the "Console" tab

    • Step 4: Enter bdthemes = {};

    How to remove bad CSS from the CSS tab manually?

    Now you might have inserted CSS and can't remove it no more because of it here is how to remove it.

    • Step 1: Do CTRL+SHIFT+I on your keyboard

    • Step 2: Click the "Console" tab

    • Step 3: Enter $('#customcss').empty()

    • Step 4: Now go back to the Custom CSS tab remove the CSS and uncheck "Live Update" and "Autosave"

    • Step 5: Restart Discord by doing CTRL+R

    No themes showing up in BetterDiscord?

    Please follow these steps to get your themes showing up again!

    • Step 1: Make sure you saved the theme to %appdata%\betterdiscord\themes\

    • Step 2: Make sure the themes file extension is .theme.css

    • Step 3: Make sure the themes 1st line of CSS contains the META tag

    • Step 4: Head back to Discord and CTRL+R on your keyboard

    • Step 5: If step 4 didn't work please reinstall BetterDiscord

    No plugins showing up in BetterDiscord?

    Please follow these steps to get your Plugins showing up again!

    • Step 1: Make sure you saved the plugin to %appdata%\betterdiscord\plugins\

    • Step 2: Make sure the plugins file extension is .plugin.js

    • Step 3: Make sure the themes 1st line of Javascript contains the META tag

    • Step 4: Head back to Discord and CTRL+R on your keyboard

    • Step 5: If step 4 didn't work please reinstall BetterDiscord

    How to change the background image of a transparent theme?

    Here are the steps to get your theme's background changed to your liking!

    • Step 1: Open the theme file with a text editor (Not Notepad)

    • Step 2: Search for .app and .callout-backdrop in the CSS. In some cases they might be in the same line of CSS

    • Step 3: Replace the link(s) with your image link. Please note that the link needs to be HTTPS:// and end with a file extension such as png,jpg,gif etc.

    • Step 4: Now finally restart Discord (CTRL+R) and you will see your background!

    When will BetterDiscord emotes be updated?

    BTTV emotes are updated weekly by EhsanKia from the BetterDiscord server

    • You can check here if it has been updated the file is called emotedata_bttv.json

    Why are some emotes not showing up?

    We as a community hate emotes getting in our sentences without wanting to put them there so we have decided to blacklisted them.

    • 3 letter emotes are blacklisted. LUL converts to LUUL

    • Common words such as STOP! are blacklisted

    What are Macs directories?!

    I forgot about Mac users! I'm sorry everything is the same for Mac users other than where you install Themes and Plugins.

    • Themes: ~/Library/Preferences/BetterDiscord/themes

    • Plugins: ~/Library/Preferences/BetterDiscord/plugins
